Cornell Satellite Remote Sensing Training Program

Application Instructions

Step 1. Determine if the Class Will Meet Your Goals

a. Review information found on the Program Home Page, look through the Program Syllabus and Overview links found in the menu bar above.

b. Send an e-mail to Bruce Monger (bcm3@cornell.edu) and briefly explain your present academic/research interests and what you hope to gain by taking the training program. The purpose of this e-mail is to start a dialog to determine if the class will meet your expectations. An e-mail response will be sent confirming the appropriateness of the training program.

c. If it is determined that the training program is appropriate for your goals (based on Step 1b), then go ahead with making payment to confirm your spot in the program.

Step 2. Program Payment

If Paying by Credit Card:

a. Click on this Cornell Business Services link to process your credit card payment online. Make payment in the amount of $2,800.

b. After your payment has been successfully processed, send Bruce Monger (bcm3@cornell.edu) a short note to notify him that your payment has been completed.

If Paying by Bank Wire Transfer:

International students can either pay by credit card (as per instructions above) or by using a bank wire transfer method. To make a bank wire transfer, follow these steps. Make payment in the amount of $2,800.

a. Download these Bank Wire Instructions (excel spreadsheet).

b. Fill in the requested information (red font) on the instruction sheet.

c. Bring the completed information to your bank to make the wire transfer.

d. After a successful wire transfer, send the completed information sheet to Bruce Monger (bcm3@cornell.edu) for record keeping.

Step 3. Housing

Campus housing reservations and payment is done through the Cornell Conference Services Group (see the registration link below).

Note: housing reservations are done independently of course registration and can be made anytime prior to your arrival on June 4.

Housing costs are: $52 per night plus a one-time administrative fee of $32 and applicable sales tax.

Registration for Campus Housing is done online--> HERE

*NOTE: Campus dormitory housing is not required. If you wish, you may use an alternative housing option such as hotel, short-term summer sublet or work with other participants to find shared housing. See housing options at the bottom of the course Overview page.

Application Deadline

The application deadline is May 1, 2017. Late applications are very welcome if space remains available. The course is limited to 20 participants.
